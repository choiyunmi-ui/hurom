FEATURES

HH Series

ONE STEP CLOSER TO NATURE – TWICE AS SLOW, TWICE AS GOOD

The 2nd Generation of Hurom Juicers preserve the natural taste and nutrition of fruits and vegetables by gently squeezing and pressing them through its leading-edge Slow Squeezing Technology’ instead of grinding them like other juicers.

GLOBALLY INNOVATIVE 2ND GENERATION SLOW SQUEEZING TECHNOLOGY (SST™) BY HUROM

Hurom’s innovative, and patented second generation SST™ technology features a new twin-winged auger that squeezes ingredients more thoroughly than before for greater amounts of mooth, delicious juice.

ENJOY ENRICHED NATURAL TASTE AND NUTRITION

Hurom’s patented SST™ technology squeezes ingredients twice as slowly as some other juicers, extracting the most juice possible, with full of natural taste and nutrition.

EASILY MIX A WIDE VARIETY OF INGREDIENTS

The juice cap makes it easy to mix an assortment of ingredients within the chamber so there is no need to stir after pouring. Create a variety of mixed, juice using preferred ingredients, as well as soy or nut milk, and even baby food.

MAKE JUICE THE WAY YOU WANT WITH A CONTROL LEVER

Our 2nd Generation Hurom Juicer controls the discharging pressure when making juice with a new control lever, allowing customers to determine the consistency of their personal juices. Users can choose juice with more or less pulp depending on their preferences.

CLEAN AND HYGIENIC USE

Use the Juice Cap for drip-free pouring directly into your glass and to prevent spillage and use the Hopper Lid to keep the juicer dust-free and to prevent foreign substances from entering the chamber when not in use

EASY CLEANING

Use the advanced self-cleaning feature to conveniently rinse the inside of the chamber.

EASY CLEANING

Close the juice cap and run water through for simple rinse cleaning.

CLOG-REDUCING SPINNING BRUSH

The spinning brush cleans the chamber and strainer simultaneously, to reduce clogging and increase juicing efficiency.

SOLIDLY BUILT WITH LOW NOISE AND LITTLE VIBRATION

Designed for home use, the slow-rotating motor makes little noise and vibration and guarantees high durability.
