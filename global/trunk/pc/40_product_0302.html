EXPLODED VIEW

Hopper
Designed to prevent human hands from entering the machine, ensuring safe use.
Ultem Auger
Precisely made with a strong and durable new material, Ultem, the auger sqeezes juice.
Strainer
Two types of interchangeable strainers, depending on purpose and ingredients.
Pulp Outlet
Emits residue after squeezing.
Juice Cap
Simply open or close the juice cap for drip-free pouring and to prevent spillage.
Spinning Brush
The inner spinning brush rotates during juicing to mix juice and to clean the chamber and strainer simultaneously.
Safety Unit
The magnetic safety unit allows the machine to operate only when it is accurately assembled.
Hopper Lid
Measure right in the chamber through transparent window and the integrated measuring scale.
Switch
Move ingredients up or down or change the rotation of the machine with the press of a switch.
