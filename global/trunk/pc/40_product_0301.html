FEATURES

HF Series

HUROM OFFERS MORE OF NATURE WITH SLOW SQUEEZING TECHNOLOGY

Hurom’s premium model is unrivalled for maintaining natural taste and nutrition by squeezing raw materials with the globally-recognized innovation of Slow Squeezing Technology(SST™).

NATURAL TASTE AND NUTRITION WITH HUROM’S INNOVATIVE TECHNOLOGY

Hurom’s exclusive ultem auger squeezes natural ingredients into healthy, nutritious juice. Stainless materials boast outstanding durability, hygiene and a formidable capacity of 16.9 oz.

EASILY MIX A WIDE VARIETY OF INGREDIENTS

The juice cap makes it easy to mix an assortment of ingredients within the chamber so there is no need to stir after pouring. Create a variety of mixed, juice using preferred ingredients, as well as soy or nut milk, and even baby food.

CLEAN AND HYGIENIC USE

Use the Juice Cap for drip-free pouring directly into your glass and to prevent spillage and use the Hopper Lid to keep the juicer dust-free and to prevent foreign substances from entering the chamber when not in use

EASY CLEANING

Use the advanced self-cleaning feature to conveniently rinse the inside of the chamber.

EASY CLEANING

Close the juice cap and run water through for simple rinse cleaning.

CLOG-REDUCING SPINNING BRUSH

The spinning brush cleans the chamber and strainer simultaneously, to reduce clogging and increase juicing efficiency.

SOLIDLY BUILT WITH LOW NOISE AND LITTLE VIBRATION

Designed for home use, the slow-rotating motor makes little noise and vibration and guarantees high durability.
