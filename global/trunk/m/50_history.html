HUROM

Hurom is a compound word of “human” and “ii-rom” the Korean word for “benefit”, meaning “Giving benefit to the people”. Hurom’s mission is to have an entrepreneurial spirit in creating a product that contributes to the health of humankind. HUMAN + II-ROM
